El Taco Riendo

Review by Sally Chen

Full Review: "Everything on El Taco Riendo's menu is incredible. I've ordered multiple variations of their taco trio, ranging from chicken to brisket to pork to tofu tacos - they have it all. Anything you want in a taco, they can make for you. I love the chill atmosphere of the restaurant. It's a great place to kick it with friends on the weekend; they also have an amazing drink selection. I'd recommend the house sangria - but don't have more than two because they're quite strong :) I'd highly recommend this to anyone who is taco obsessed like me!" - 01/15/2017
