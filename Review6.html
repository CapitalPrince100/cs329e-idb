Wildwood Kitchen

Review by James Connelly

Full Review: "Before I start harping on the quality of the food, I will applaud Wildwood for having tremendous customer service. My waiter Sarah was always quick and responsive to my requests and showed enthusiasm and genuine concern for my experience at the restaurant. But beyond that, I will say, Wildwood is nothing more than subpar barbeque. I ordered the brisket and pork ribs, and both of my platters came out at room temperature and also quite dry. I asked for more sauce and also more seasoning, but neither could fix the problem that was the preparation of the meat. I was disappointed with my experience because I had received many recommendations saying that Wildwood would be the best bang for your buck in town, but I have to say, you get what you pay for. While it isn't a wildly overpriced BBQ spot, it doesn't exceed my expectations. Maybe I'll try ordering something else next time." -4/15/2016
