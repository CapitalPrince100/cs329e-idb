    Beef wasn't well cooked...way too chewy and unseasoned. Probably wouldn't return again. But everything else wasn't bad...I will say, for vegetarians, it does have a redeeming factor: they do have a temporary buy-one-get-one deal for vegetarian tacos during certain hours. So perhaps if you're looking for a cheap, vegetarian meal in NYC for you and a friend...maybe you can stop by here. Just don't get the beef.
