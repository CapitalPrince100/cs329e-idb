VertsKebap

Review by John Anderson

Full Review: "Their Kebaps are really good and healthy. This place has changed a lot since I first started UT. Still my go to place when I need something fast, but have been a little disappointed in the prices increases. In particular, I really enjoy their beef and gyro wrap. Having lived a majority of my life in Germany where Doner Kebaps are a staple food, I can attest that the meat is fresh and pleasingly authentic. I would recommend anyone who wants to try some quick and tasty German food to come here!" - 2/1/2017
